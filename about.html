About BibleFluent

A ministry of the Pineda Amador family serving the Church of Christ in Latin America and around the world

BibleFluent was born as part of the digital ministry of the Pineda Amador family, with the firm purpose of equipping the Church of Christ in the study of biblical and modern languages using the Scriptures themselves as the learning text.

We believe that knowing Hebrew, Koine Greek, and other languages is not an end in itself, but a powerful tool to deepen God's Word, improve teaching, and spread the gospel with greater precision and passion.

All our content is free, with a practical and accessible approach. We do not charge for knowledge; it is a gift we have received and wish to share with the entire brotherhood.

"For I am not ashamed of the gospel, for it is the power of God for salvation to everyone who believes" (Romans 1:16).

Mission & Vision

  • Teach biblical and modern languages through the Bible.
  • Serve the Church of Christ in Latin America and worldwide.
  • Offer completely free, high-quality resources.
  • Strengthen unity and spiritual growth through studying the Scriptures in their original languages.

Vision: That every disciple of Christ may access the riches of the original text without economic or linguistic barriers.
