Privacy Policy

Personal data protection at BibleFluent – Christian ministry

At BibleFluent, part of the digital ministry of the Pineda Amador family, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website.

1. Information we collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal information: Name, email address (when you subscribe to our newsletter or contact us).
  • Usage data: Pages visited, time spent, and browsing preferences (via cookies).
  • Contact information: When you send us a message through the contact form or by email.

2. Use of information

We use your information to:

  • Respond to your inquiries, prayer requests, or requests.
  • Send you free resources, devotionals, and ministry updates (if you have subscribed).
  • Improve our website and learning experience.
  • Protect the security and integrity of our services.

3. Cookies and similar technologies

This site uses cookies to remember your language preferences and analyze traffic. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, although some features may be affected.

4. Sharing information with third parties

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties, except when necessary to comply with the law or protect our rights. We use third-party services such as Google Analytics to analyze traffic; these services have their own privacy policies.

5. Links to other websites

Our site may contain links to external resources (IDCLATAM, Pineda Amador, PayPal, etc.). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. We recommend reading their policies.

6. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure.

7. Children under 13

BibleFluent does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with data, please contact us to delete it.

8. Your rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data by writing to us at juancanales5019@gmail.com. You may also unsubscribe from our emails at any time.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy occasionally. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new version on this page. The date of the last update is indicated below.
